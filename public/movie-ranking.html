The Best Movies According To Ayush

My top 3 movies of all time

Interstellar

Overall a great movie. Phenomenal casting, stunning visuals and engaging soundtrack. Nolan once again nails it to perfection

Oppenheimer

One of the finest documentaries made on renowned Physicist - Dr. Robert J Oppenheimer. Cillian Murphy really gives a record breaking performance.

La La Land

A musical based romance movie which showcases the bittersweet elements of life. The chemistry between Ryan Gosling and Emma Watson feels natural. Overall a must watch for hopeless romantics.
